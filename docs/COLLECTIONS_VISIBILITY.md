# Collections - Visibility Levels

Collections in TyneBase have three visibility levels that control who can access them.

## Visibility Levels

| Level | Who Can View | Who Can Edit | Who Can Delete |
|-------|--------------|--------------|----------------|
| **Private** (default) | Author + invited members only | Author + editors | Author only |
| **Team** | All organisation members | Author only | Author only |
| **Public** | All organisation members | Author only | Author only |

## Private Collections

Private collections are **user-private by default** — only the author can see and manage them unless they explicitly invite others.

### Sharing Private Collections

Authors can invite specific team members with two roles:
- **Viewer**: Can view documents in the collection
- **Editor**: Can add/remove documents from the collection

Only the author can:
- Invite or remove members
- Change visibility settings
- Delete the collection

## Changing Visibility

When changing a private collection to team or public:
- Existing invited members will retain access
- All organisation members will gain view access
- Only the author retains edit/delete permissions

## Best Practices

- Use **Private** for personal work or draft collections
- Use **Team** for collections relevant to your department
- Use **Public** for company-wide resources
